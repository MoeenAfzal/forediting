History & Vision

The Storehouse Project, Inc. is a Christ-centered non-profit organization dedicated to serving the people of Connecticut. Our mission is to provide essential items for living and to share the unconditional love of God through the message of Jesus Christ. We embrace everyone with kindness and without judgment, upholding the values of inclusivity and compassion. Our services are accessible to all, regardless of their background, ensuring that we support our diverse community. We rely on donations to continue serving our community effectively.

History

The Storehouse Project, Inc. is a Christ-centered non-profit organization which exists to provide individuals throughout Connecticut with food and other items necessary for living, and to share with them the unconditional love of God through the gospel of Jesus Christ. We gladly receive everyone with kindness and without judgment. Our services are available to all, regardless of race, religion, gender, age, national or ethnic origin, marital status or physical ability. We welcome donations to meet the ongoing needs of the people we serve in our community.

The Storehouse Project started with a small closet sized pantry in 2000. We have since grown into a multi-program organization and in 2016, we incorporated as a 501 C 3.
